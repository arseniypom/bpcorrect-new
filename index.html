Patients

Why monitor your blood pressure at home ?

Blood pressure measured in the doctor’s office is not always accurate. And these days, monitoring your blood pressure in the safety and privacy of your home may be more preferable to going to a doctor's office. Experts agree that checking your blood pressure at home in the morning and in the evening for 7 days in a row is the best way to determine your true blood pressure.

The BPCorrect app helps you do that by:

Connecting to affordable and accurate home blood pressure monitors

Providing step-by-step instructions on how to correctly measure blood pressure

Reminding you to check your blood pressure for 7 days in a row

Summarizing your results

Providing links to information about managing your blood pressure

Allowing you to share your blood pressure measurements with your doctor

Why People Choose BPCorrect ?

The American Heart Association (AHA), the United States Preventive Services Task Force and the Million Hearts Initiative all recommend home blood pressure monitoring prior to the diagnosis of hypertension and for its management.

The BPCorrect App

  • Integrates with clinically validated, affordable home BP monitors
  • Includes a 7-day BP monitoring period recommended by the AHA
  • Reminds patients when BP measurements are due
  • Using video and text, provides step-by-step patient instructions for BP measurement
  • Includes timers for the appropriate rest intervals before and between BP measurements
  • Aggregates BP values and displays summaries for patients
  • De-emphasizes focus on individual measurements
  • Incorporates lifestyle education to lower BP
  • Sends all BP measures to the HIPAA-secure BPCorrect Clinician Portal

The BPCorrect Clinician Portal

  • Provides reports on individual patient’s progress on obtaining sufficient measurements to determine an accurate assessment of the patient’s blood pressure
  • Displays the average blood pressure value and its interpretation based on AHA guidelines once a sufficient number of measurements have been obtained
  • Includes a dashboard highlighting patients with average blood pressures outside of range
  • Includes ability to create accounts for clinical staff members to review blood pressure measurements
  • Includes timer to record time spent per patient to facilitate billing using remote monitoring codes (CPT 99453, 99454, 99457, 99458)
